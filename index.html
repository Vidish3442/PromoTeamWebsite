Is the Nobel Internship really free?
Yes — 100% free, always. Nobel's mission is to provide equal education and growth opportunities for everyone, regardless of financial background. There are no hidden fees, ever.
Who can apply? What's the minimum age?
Anyone aged 13 or above. You need a laptop or desktop (no phones/tablets), a headset with mic, webcam, stable internet, and ability to commit 4+ hours per week (8+ for the weekday program).
Can I join from a Tier-2 or Tier-3 city in India?
Absolutely. The entire program is online via video meetings — you can join from anywhere in India as long as you have a stable internet connection and a laptop.
What do I receive at the end?
A verified internship certificate after completing Fundamentals. Going further earns you a LinkedIn recommendation, recommendation letter, and real portfolio work from your Nobel team projects.
How is Nobel different from other online internships?
In Nobel you actively facilitate courses, lead cohorts, speak publicly, and work in real global teams — not just watch videos. It's a learner-to-leader model that gives you genuine leadership experience.
What is the India Promo Team?
The India Promo Team is a group of Nobel interns and graduates spreading awareness across India through college outreach, social media, events, and content. To join, you first need to apply and complete the Nobel Fundamentals program — then you're eligible to be part of the India team.
Can I apply while in college or school?
Yes — most Nobel interns are college students or school students. The flexible weekday/weekend schedule is designed to fit around academic commitments.
